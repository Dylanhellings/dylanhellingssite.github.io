About Me

I am a student at The Pennsylvania State University studying Security & Risk Analysis with a focus in Information & Cyber Security. In addition to my major, I am pursuing a minor in Information Sciences & Technology. Through my studies, I have gained a healthy understanding of risk analysis in a security context and several important elements of cyber security, notably network security and cyber threat defense.

I have always placed tremendous value in education and teaching others. I plan to use my knowledge and experience not only within my professional career but most importantly to educate those who do not understand the importance of information security in the increasingly technologically involved world we live in.

Stemming from my love of learning and teaching others, I have made a concerted effort to expand my education and experiences into different disciplines rather than confine myself within the scope of my primary interests. Through self-education and experience with freelance work, I have become proficient in other subject areas such as graphic design, digital marketing, and computer hardware. Through various experiences as a student, an intern, and through self-education I have also gained a working understanding of web design, cyber liability insurance, and entrepreneurship.
